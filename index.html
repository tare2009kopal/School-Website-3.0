What's New

  • Cricket is being introduced to Fallon's Sports

    This year, Fallon is introducing cricket to the many sports you can play here at fallon middle. There are many sports teams here including soccer, basketball, baseball football, track and field, volleyball, etc. Cricket is one of the most suggested sports, so this year, the staff decided to incorporate it in Fallon's extracurricular activity.

  • Mr. Ken Rocha is moving away from Fallon Middle

    This year, Fallon's principal Mr. Ken Rocha is moving away from fallon middle school. A new temporary principal will be taking over until a permanent replacement is found. Good luck in your new home Mr. Rocha! We'll miss you!
